## Gap analysis and market saturation assessment

### What is already ÔÇťsaturatedÔÇŁ vs what appears underbuilt

Forecasting platforms are strong on **rollups, drill-down to deals, and AI risk/score narratives**, including explicit ÔÇťforecast submission ÔÇŽ overrides, and notesÔÇŁ and ÔÇťopportunity-level overridesÔÇŁ as forecast-solution requirements. ţłÇciteţłéturn28view0ţłéturn36view0ţłéturn38view1ţłü Commission platforms are strong on **rep statements, tracing/audit, dispute workflows, and estimators**, including ÔÇťcommission tracing functionalityÔÇŁ and ÔÇťcommission estimatorÔÇŁ positioning. ţłÇciteţłéturn8view0ţłéturn6view0ţłü Signal engines are strong on **activity/conversation-driven risk signals**, such as deal risk signals for engagement/keywords and ML-driven deal likelihood scores. ţłÇciteţłéturn37view0ţłéturn38view1ţłéturn3view0ţłü

What is materially less common (and is your likely wedge) is **closing the loop** between these categories:

1. **Governance loop:** a formal, auditable _forecast governance state machine_ (submit/approve/freeze) that is not merely ÔÇťrollups and scoring,ÔÇŁ but an explicit process that operations can enforce.
